Hello world wide web!

Welcome to computer-conscience, a set of plug-and-play modules you can use in your core CS curriculum courses.

These modules teach key technical content, but also prompt students to think about the ethical implications of their technology.

Peruse the links below to see if one of the modules is a good fit for your class. You can also check out the wiki for additional information and ideas contributed by other users of this site.
